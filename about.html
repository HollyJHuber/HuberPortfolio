About Holly J. Huber

Holly is a developer and programmer from Muskegon, Michigan.

After graduating summa cum laude from Albion College, Holly moved to Boston where she started a Mac-based computer training company; her clients included Harvard University, Fidelity Investments, and ITT Sheraton.

Holly relocated to Hawaii, where she expanded to a Mac computer-services business called Island InfoTech.

After years working for the Honolulu Marathon, Holly formed Sports Media Productions with a business partner. There she developed an automated system for producing personalized DVDs for participants in large marathons.

Holly recently returned to West Michigan from Hawaii. She is currently upgrading her skills by programming the Harmonagon — a free web app for music education, using Javascript, HTML and CSS — for Radical Data & Media LLC.
