About Holly J. Huber

Holly is a developer and programmer from Muskegon, Michigan.

After graduating summa cum laude from Albion College, Holly moved to Boston where she started a small computer training business; her clients included Harvard University, Fidelity Investments, and ITT Sheraton.

Holly relocated to Hawaii, where she developed an automated system for producing personalized DVDs for participants in the Ironman Triathlon, and the Boston, Honolulu, Los Angeles, New York City and other large marathons.

Holly recently returned to West Michigan. She is currently upgrading her skills by programming the Harmonagon, a free web app for music education, using Javascript, HTML and CSS.
